Skyward Drift - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Introduction

Skyward Drift ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed as an indie project. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Skyward Drift itself does not directly collect any personal information. However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information:

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Services
The app may use Google Play Services for in-app purchases (remove ads feature). Google Play Services may collect device information. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Game settings (audio preferences, language selection, game progress) are stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit or store this data on any external server.

Advertising

The app displays ads through Google AdMob, including banner ads and rewarded video ads. You may purchase a "Remove Ads" option to disable all advertisements permanently. Ad-related data collection is governed by Google's privacy practices.

In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. All purchase transactions are processed through Google Play and are subject to Google's terms and privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Skyward Drift is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app's ad content rating is set to "General" (G).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
